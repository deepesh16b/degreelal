We Are Degreelal Sweets

It is located in Khandwa, Madhya Pradesh, India, is a renowned restaurant crafting delectable sweets and flavorful namkeens since 1980. With a rich culinary heritage, our traditional recipes are prepared using authentic ingredients and love. From popular jalebis to aromatic sev and bhujia, our menu offers a delightful journey through Indian flavors. Experience our warm ambiance and exceptional service for a memorable dining experience. Join us & indulge in authentic Indian sweets and namkeens, and immerse yourself in the true essence of our culinary heritage.
